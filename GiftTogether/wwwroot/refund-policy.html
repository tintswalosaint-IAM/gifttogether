🎁 Neo GiftTogether

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Nature of Contributions

Neo GiftTogether is a collaborative gifting platform. Contributors make voluntary monetary contributions toward gift goals created by registry owners (creators). All contributions are voluntary gifts made directly to the creator via Paystack, our payment processor.

2. No Refunds on Contributions

Because contributions are voluntary gifts made to a specific person's gift registry, all contributions are non-refundable once payment has been confirmed. Neo GiftTogether does not hold, escrow, or control any funds — payments are processed and settled directly to the creator's bank account by Paystack.

Contributors are advised to:

  • Only contribute to registries created by people they personally know and trust.
  • Verify the registry details before completing payment.
  • Contact the registry creator directly if they have concerns about a contribution.

3. Disputes

Neo GiftTogether does not mediate disputes between contributors and registry creators. If a contributor believes a payment was made in error or fraudulently, they should:

  • Contact their bank or card issuer to initiate a chargeback if applicable.
  • Contact Paystack support at paystack.com/contact.

4. Registry Cancellation

Registry creators may delete their registry at any time. Deletion of a registry does not trigger automatic refunds to contributors. Creators are responsible for communicating any changes to their contributors directly.

5. Platform Fees

Neo GiftTogether charges a flat service fee of R2.00 per contribution, in addition to Paystack's standard processing fee. These fees are non-refundable as they cover the cost of payment processing and platform operation.
